About Us

At Venkateshwara Enterprises, we believe in the power of continuity. Since our founding in 2016, our mission has been to deliver robust and dependable power backup solutions that ensure uninterrupted operations for both businesses and homes—even in the most demanding environments.

Our legacy in the power supply industry dates back to 2013, when we operated as Power Source Technologies. Between 2013 and 2016, we built a solid foundation of technical expertise and customer trust. In 2016, we rebranded as Venkateshwara Enterprises to expand our services and strengthen our commitment to delivering comprehensive power solutions.

With decades of combined experience, our expert team provides end-to-end power services—from consultation and installation to maintenance and ongoing support. We don’t just sell UPS systems; we deliver peace of mind through reliable, tailored power solutions backed by exceptional service.

At Venkateshwara Enterprises, your power is our priority.
